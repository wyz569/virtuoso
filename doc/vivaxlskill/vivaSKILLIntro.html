Virtuoso Visualization and Analysis XL SKILL functions

Virtuoso Visualization and Analysis XL SKILL functions are grouped into following categories according to their purpose.

Category Description

Waveform Window Functions

Lets you perform various operations on a subwindow of a Waveform window.

These functions have the prefix awv.

Results Browser Functions

Lets you perform various operations on Results Browser.

These functions have the prefix rdb.

Calculator Functions

Lets you perform various operations on Calculator.

You can also perform various calculations on the results of transient and ac analysis and plot outputs using these functions.

RF Functions

Lets you perform various calculations on results of Spectre RF analyses and plot their outputs.

Usually, these functions have the prefix rf.
