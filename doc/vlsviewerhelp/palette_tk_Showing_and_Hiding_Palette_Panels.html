Showing and Hiding Palette Assistant Panels

You can choose to display and hide the Layers, Objects, and Grids panels in the Palette assistant according to your requirements.

To display a panel in the Palette assistant, use either of the following ways:

  • Right-click any panel title bar and choose the panel you want to display.
  • Use the pteMapWindow SKILL function to specify the name of the Palette panel to be displayed.

To hide a panel in the Palette assistant, use either of the following ways:

  • Right-click any panel title bar and choose the panel you want to hide.
  • Use the pteUnmapWindow SKILL function to specify the name of the Palette panel to be hidden.
