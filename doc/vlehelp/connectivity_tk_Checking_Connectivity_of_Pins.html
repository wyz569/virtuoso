Checking Connectivity of Pins

To see whether pins on a net are defined for external connection:

  1. In the layout window, choose Connectivity – Pins – Must Connect.
  2. Press F3 to open the Connectivity – Must Connected Pins form.
    The form appears, showing all the nets that include terminals, which are possible connections to devices external to the device.
  3. Click any of the nets shown in the form.
    Flight lines for the net appear on the canvas, showing whether the terminals are defined as must-connect, strong-connect, or weak-connect.
