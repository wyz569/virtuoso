Remove Shape From Net Form

Use the Remove Shape From Net form to remove the selected shape from the net.

Field Description

Net Name

Shows the net of the selected shape. If more than one shape is selected, Net Name shows the net of the first shape. If the net name shown is not the net you want the shapes deleted from, type the correct net name.
