viaAutoViaAlignment

layout viaAutoViaAlignment cyclic {"auto" | "top-left" | "top-center" | "top-right" | "center-left" | "center-center" | "center-right" | "bottom-left" | "bottom-center" | "bottom-right" | "edge-align" | "two-edge-align" | "two-edge-align-center"}

Description

Specifies the alignment of the via.

The default value is auto .

GUI Equivalent

Command:

Create – Via; Mode: Auto

Field:

Via Alignment

Examples

envGetVal("layout" "viaAutoViaAlignment")
envSetVal("layout" "viaAutoViaAlignment" 'cyclic "top-left")
envSetVal("layout" "viaAutoViaAlignment" 'cyclic "center-left")
envSetVal("layout" "viaAutoViaAlignment" 'cyclic "bottom-right")
envSetVal("layout" "viaAutoViaAlignment" 'cyclic "two-edge-align")
