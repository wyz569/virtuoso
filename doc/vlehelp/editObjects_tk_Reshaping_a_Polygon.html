Reshaping a Polygon

To reshape a polygon:

  1. In the layout window, choose Edit – Advanced – Reshape.
  2. Select a polygon on the canvas.
  3. Press F3 to open the Reshape form.
  4. From Mode, select line as the reshape mode.
  5. Create the new section of the polygon on the canvas.
  6. Double-click when you are finished entering points.
  7. Click the middle mouse button to toggle between highlighting the new shape and highlighting both the old and new shape.
  8. When the shape you want is highlighted, click on the canvas.
