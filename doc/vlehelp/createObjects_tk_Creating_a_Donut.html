Creating a Donut

To create a donut:

  1. ln the layout window, from the Layers panel, select the layer on which you want to create a donut.
  2. Choose Create – Shape – Donut.
  3. Click at a point on the canvas where you want the center of the donut. Alternatively, you can specify the points of the donut using the Enter Points form.
  4. Click at a point where you want the inner edge of the donut.
  5. Click at a point where you want the outer edge of the donut.
