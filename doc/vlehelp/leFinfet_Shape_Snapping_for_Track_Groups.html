Shape Snapping for Track Groups

Snap pattern shapes of the snapPatternDef snap to their period lines. Shape snapping for track groups is supported both for global and local snap pattern definitions.

For shape-to-snap-pattern snapping, the shape is snapped by having the lower or left edge of the shape enclose a track by the trackEnclosure value. The shape is snapped to the nearest track. To move a snapped shape from one track group to another, you need to move the pointer by at least half the track group spacing.
