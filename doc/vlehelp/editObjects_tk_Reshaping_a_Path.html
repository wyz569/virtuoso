Reshaping a Path

To reshape a path:

  1. In the layout window, choose Edit – Advanced – Reshape.
  2. Select a path on the canvas.
  3. Press F3 to open the Reshape form.
  4. From Mode, select line as the reshape mode.
  5. Starting from the centerline, create the new section of the path on the canvas.
  6. Double-click when you are finished entering points.
  7. Click the middle mouse button to toggle between highlighting the reshaped path options.
  8. When the shape you want is highlighted, click on the canvas.
