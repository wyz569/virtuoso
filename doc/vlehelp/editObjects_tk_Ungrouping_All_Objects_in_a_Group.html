Ungrouping All Objects in a Group

To ungroup all objects from a group:

  1. In the layout window, choose Edit – Group – Ungroup.
    You are prompted to point at the group to ungroup it.
  2. On the canvas, click the group you want to ungroup.
    Alternatively, you can select the group and right-click it. Then, select Ungroup from the Group shortcut menu.
    The group is ungrouped. The line surrounding the group is deleted, and no longer appears when you place the pointer over the objects that were in the group.

When you ungroup objects from a group, the child object of the group is not deleted after you ungroup.
