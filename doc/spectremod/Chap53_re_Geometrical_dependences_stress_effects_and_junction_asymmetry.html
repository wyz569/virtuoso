Asymmetric Junctions

After the calculations described Scaling Equations and Stress Model, local parameters are clipped according to the min/max values of the parameters.

Then, if the switch parameter SWJUNASYM is equal to 0, drain and source junctions are assumed symmetrical and all parameters related to the drain junction are overwritten by their source side counterparts.

If SWJUNASYM=0:

If SWJUNASYM = 1, the parameters related to the drain side are kept unchanged.

If some of the drain side parameters are not specified in the model card, they take their default value and not the value of their source side counterpart.
