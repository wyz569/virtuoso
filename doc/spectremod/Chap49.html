BSIM-IMG

BSIM-IMG is a SPICE compact model for modeling the electrical characteristics of common gate MG structures, developed by UC Berkeley. The latest version is 102.80.

The BSIM-IMG models the independent double-gate structure as a four terminal device, containing the source(s), drain(d), front gate(fg), and back gate(bg) terminals. The two gates (fg, bg) are allowed to have different workfunctions ( ) and dielectric thicknesses (Tox1, Tox2). They can also be biased separately at different voltages.
