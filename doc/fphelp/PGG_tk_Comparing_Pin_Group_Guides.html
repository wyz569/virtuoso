Comparing Pin Group Guides

You can compare the pin group guides in two different cellviews using the Compare Pin Group Guides form.

To compare the pin group guides:

  1. Select Floorplan – Pin Group Guides – Compare.
    In Layout EXL and higher tiers, choose Plan – Pin Planning – Compare Pin Group Guides.
  2. The Compare Pin Group Guides form is displayed. Use this form to compare the pin group guides in two different cellviews.
  3. Click Browse and select the cellview or type the lib:cell:view of the source cellview.
