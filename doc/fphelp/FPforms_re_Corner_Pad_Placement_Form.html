Corner Pad Placement Form

Use the Corner Pad Placement form to insert corner cells in the four corners of a design.

Field Description

Place Corner Pads On Inner Row (applicable for Two Rows Per Side)

Places the corner pads aligned to the inner rows in a two-row-per-side layout design.

Upper Left, Upper Right, Lower Left, and Lower Right

Inserts four corners on the design in which corner cells are placed.

Instance Name

Specifies the instances to be used as corner cells.

Orientation

Specifies the orientation of the selected instance. You can choose from eight legal orientations in the drop-down menu (R0, R90, R180, R270, MY, MYR90, MX, or MXR90).
