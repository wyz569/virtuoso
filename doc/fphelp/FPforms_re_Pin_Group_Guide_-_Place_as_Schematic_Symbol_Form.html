Pin Group Guide - Place as Schematic/Symbol Form

Use the Pin Group Guide - Place as Schematic/Symbol form to generate pin group guides and place pins as per their relative positions in the schematic or symbol view.

Field Description

Schematic

Specifies that the pins in the pin group guide are to be placed as per their counterparts in the corresponding schematic view.

Symbol

Specifies that the pins in the pin group guide are to be placed as per their counterparts in the corresponding symbol view.

Library Name, Cell Name, and View Name

Specifies the cellview in which the pin group guide is to be generated.
