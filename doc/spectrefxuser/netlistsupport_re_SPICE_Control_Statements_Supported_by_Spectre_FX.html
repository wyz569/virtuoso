SPICE Control Statements Supported by Spectre FX

Spectre FX supports the following SPICE control statements:

  • .alter
  • .connect
  • .data
  • .end
  • .endl
  • .ends or .eom
  • .global
  • .ic
  • .if and .else
  • .include
  • .lib
  • .malias
  • .model
  • .nodeset
  • .op
  • .options
  • .param
  • .subckt or .macro
  • .temp
  • .tran
