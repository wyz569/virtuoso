Displaying and Hiding Voltus-XFi Results Browser

To display the results browser in the Virtuoso Layout window:

  • Choose Window – Assistants – Voltus-XFi Results Browser.

Once selected, the results browser assistant is added as a docked assistant pane within the current layout window. By default, the assistant is positioned at the right side of the session window.

To hide the assistant:

  • Choose Window – Assistants - Voltus-XFi Results Browser.
