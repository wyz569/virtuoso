Highlighting a Device on Schematic

While analyzing data in the mismatch contribution table, you can highlight a device on the schematic to view its exact location. This can help in identifying and modifying a device in the schematic to improve the variance data, if required.

To highlight a device on the schematic:

  • Right-click a row and choose Highlight on Schematic.
    Alternatively, you can press H on the keyboard.
