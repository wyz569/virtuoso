Advantages of Advanced Optimization

Advantages of using advanced optimization in ADE Assembler are as follows:

  • Lets you use different optimizers as per the need.
    • Comes with various algorithms, such as particle swarm optimization (PSO), Dakota, Optuna, and other surrogate model-based optimizers.
    • Supports custom optimization algorithms that you can write on your own.
    • Allows further tuning of hyperparameters of global optimizers (legacy optimizers).
  • Provides multi-language support, C++ and Python.
  • Provides better data management to share results using web server.
