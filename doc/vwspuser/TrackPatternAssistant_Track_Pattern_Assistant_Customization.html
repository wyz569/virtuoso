Track Pattern Assistant Customization

You can customize the Track Pattern assistant in the following ways:

  • You can control the visibility of the Filter section by expanding or collapsing it using the icon, .
  • You can also click and drag the horizontal section divider to control the amount of information shown in the Filter section.
  • Click and drag table column headers to the left or right to reorder columns.
  • Click table column dividers to resize the columns.
  • Click the Layer/Def column to reorder the layers in an ascending or descending order.
