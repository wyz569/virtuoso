Make Cell – Connected

The made cell reflects the correct logical connectivity in terms of the instance terminals, terminals and nets, shapes, and their assignments.

Set makeCellPreserveConn to false, if you do not want connectivity of the instances inside the made cell to be preserved.

To view the connections between the transparent made cell instance and the corresponding schematic instances, you can probe the instances and nets across the schematic and layout views, as shown in the figure below.
