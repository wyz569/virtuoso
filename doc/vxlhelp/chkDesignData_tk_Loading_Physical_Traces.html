Loading Physical Traces

To load physical traces from a previously saved cellview to your current cellview:

  1. Choose Connectivity – Net Tracer – Load Physical Traces.
    The Load Traced Nets View Form opens.
  2. Specify the view name to use for loading the visible traces.
  3. (Optional) Choose Replace Traced Nets to replace the displayed traces in the current cellview with the traces being loaded.
  4. Click OK.
    The previously saved traces from the selected cellview are loaded to the current cellview.
