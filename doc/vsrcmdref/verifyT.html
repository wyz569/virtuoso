Verify Commands

The Verify commands let you examine connectivity in the design and check for the following types of violations.

  • Net connectivity (opens, shorts, loops, dangles, weakly connected pin shapes, rule changes, crossings and port shorts)
  • Shape-based such as spacing, width, minimum area, minimum enclosed area, minimum edge length, grid alignment, and vias
  • Metal density
  • Process antenna

The following is a list of the verify commands.

check_connectivity
